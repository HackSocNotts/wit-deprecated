Frequently Asked Questions

What is Women In Tech about?

Women In Tech is a day to learn about the women currently working in the technology industry. It's aimed at students of any age, from any background who have an interest in technology as a career or as a hobby.

Can guys come too?

YES! Although the emphasis is on women, the day itself is still a technology conference with amazing speakers, workshops and supporters. It's a valuable opportunity for both genders to listen to some brilliant stories and be inspired.

Where is the Women In Tech day held?

It will be held at the University of Nottingham's Jubilee Campus. It will be based in the Business School South building.

What does the day include?

In the morning we have a number of inspirational talks from notable women. After lunch there will be some mini talks, followed by a chance to network with great companies over some cake and take part in workshops lead by great people in the technology and STEM industries.

Is there a code of conduct?

Yes. We are dedicated to providing a fun, harassment-free experience for everyone. We expect all of our attendees, sponsors, volunteers, and staff to be respectful and considerate of others. They are all required to agree with this code of conduct.

Which companies will be there?

Women in Technology 2016 is brought to you by Capital One, with additional sponsorship from Intel and more companies to be announced!

Do we get food and drinks?

YES! We will provide refreshments on arrival, plus lunch and treats throughout the day. Be sure to join us in the afternoon for our Women in Technology Cake Drop!

What workshops can I take part in?

Last year we had Workshops and talks given by Capital One, Code Club, Impression, Uni of Nottingham Careers, MakeSense and more.
